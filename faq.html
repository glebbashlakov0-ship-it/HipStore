  • My Order

  • Before contacting support, check the courier tracking link from your dispatch email, confirm your delivery address is correct, and check with neighbours or any safe place around your property.

    If the parcel still cannot be found, contact us with your order number and we will investigate.

  • You may be able to cancel shortly after placing the order, but fulfilment moves quickly and cancellation is not guaranteed once processing begins.

    If cancellation is no longer possible, you can return the order after delivery using the returns process.

  • Once an order has been placed, we cannot usually add, swap, or amend products within that order. The fastest option is to place a new order for the correct item and contact support about the original one if needed.

  • Orders are sometimes cancelled when an item becomes unavailable during picking or when a payment check cannot be completed. Refund timing depends on your payment method.

  • Returns

  • Returns are generally accepted within 28 days from the date you receive your order, provided the item is in its original condition and packaging.

  • Once your return has been received and checked, refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Bank processing times vary, so the funds may take several working days to appear.

  • Sale items can usually be returned within the standard returns window unless the product page or checkout clearly states otherwise.

  • Payment & Security

  • My Account

  • You can browse freely, but having an account makes it easier to track orders, manage saved details, and move through checkout faster on future purchases.

  • Use the password recovery link on the login page. If you no longer have access to the email address on the account, contact support for help.

  • Once signed in, you can update the information stored in your account area. If something looks wrong on a live order, contact support before the parcel is dispatched.

  • Delivery

  • Delivery options and prices are shown during checkout. Timing varies by service level and destination, and tracking is available on supported shipments.

  • If store delivery or collection is available, it will appear as a selectable delivery option during checkout.

  • The courier may attempt redelivery, leave the parcel in a safe place, or hold it for collection depending on the service used. Check the tracking page for the latest status.

  • International Customers

  • Yes, international shipping can be supported. Availability, cost, and transit times depend on the destination and the courier service selected.

  • Depending on the destination, import duties, VAT, and customs fees may be charged separately by the destination country. These costs are usually outside the product price unless checkout states otherwise.

  • International orders generally take longer than UK delivery. Transit time depends on destination, customs processing, and the service chosen at checkout.
